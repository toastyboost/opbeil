Social Media Marketing

Includes working with groups in social networks: creating and posting content, organizing contests and other types of activity for the audience, attracting new subscribers, communicating with group members.
 

SEO

It is the optimization of a site to increase its position in search engines for key queries. It does not give a quick effect (the first positive changes can be seen in 3-4 months), but it allows you to get a stable result.
 

Content Marketing

Creating a vision and trusting relationship with a potential client. Generates three times more leads than paid search ads. And our research has shown that 47% of shoppers view three to five posts before purchasing a product or service.

PPC Advertising

We make a forecast of the return on advertising in advance. We select channels for your business and set up those channels where your customers are. We optimize advertising in order to achieve goals in terms of cost and number of applications or in terms of sales. We offer and test new advertising channels.

Competitor Research

Competitor analysis involves a professional study of the activities of market players, designed to provide you with up-to-date data on the methods and actions used by competitors to strengthen your position in the market. A carefully planned analysis allows one to build objective hypotheses about the trends and speed of development of the competitive environment.
